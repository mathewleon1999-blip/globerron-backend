Our Story

Jiangxi JDZ Auto Parts Co., Ltd. is one of China’s leading manufacturers of automotive shock absorbers, specializing in the research, development, and production of high‑quality suspension components. With a national‑level laboratory and fully automated production lines, our products are widely used across many vehicle brands and high‑end automotive air suspension systems.

Established in November 2017, the company has a registered capital of 20 million RMB, fixed assets of 80 million RMB, and covers an area of more than 20,000 square meters. It is a key project of the Jingdezhen High‑Tech Zone.

Our factory includes an office building, laboratory building, scientific research expert building, pipe machining workshop, welding assembly workshop, painting and packaging workshop, raw material warehouse, finished product warehouse, dormitory building, and canteen — supported by professional equipment and facilities.

We place strong emphasis on R&D innovation and quality control. JDZ currently holds 8 utility model patents, has been recognized as a high‑tech enterprise, and has passed the TS16949 quality management system certification.

With an annual output of 3.6 million automotive shock absorbers and related parts, around 80% of our products are exported globally. We are committed to becoming a trusted supplier for domestic OEMs and the international aftermarket.

Looking ahead, JDZ is developing towards the R&D and production of aviation accessories and high‑speed rail shock absorbers. We are determined to become a global manufacturer and supplier of aircraft shock absorbers, aircraft landing gear, aircraft seat shock absorbers, and locomotive and vehicle hydraulic shock absorbers.
