    FAQ — UAE Auto Parts

    Quick answers to common questions about spare parts availability, fitment checks, and enquiries in the UAE.

    Do you deliver across the UAE?
    Yes. We support enquiries and delivery coordination across the UAE. Contact us on WhatsApp with your part number and vehicle details.
    How do I confirm part compatibility?
    Select your vehicle on the website (Make/Model/Year/Engine) and share your VIN/part number if available. We verify fitment before checkout.
    Which parts do you supply?
    We supply shock absorbers, suspension parts, brakes and other components, including high-end air suspension items depending on availability.
    How fast can I get a quote?
    Usually within the same day during business hours. Add items to the enquiry cart and tap “Request Quote” or message us on WhatsApp.
